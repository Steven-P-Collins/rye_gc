Our Mission

The Rye Garden Club has dedicated itself to the ‘advancement of gardening' and the protection of the natural resources of our beautiful coastal community.

History

In 1918 the club hosted a bulb show for the public, led workshops on war gardens and canning vegetables and raised funds for the Red Cross and United Hospital. Educational programs at Rye elementary schools began in the 1940s and continue with strength today.

Community Projects

Over the years The Rye Garden Club has been responsible for planting and maintaining a few gardens around Rye. Every spring and fall we clean up, trim back and put the gardens to bed at the Square House and the Rye Library.

Events

The Rye Garden Club has offered and particiapted in numerous events over the course of its existence. Some of our finest moments have come from showcasing our work during the community garden walks, the She Flower Show, and our countless other events.
